Bionics and prosthetics

Bionics is technology that now and in the future will assist those who lose certain basic human abilities ,such as walking or vision. This technology uses biology and applies it to engineering and attempts to mimic and can seek to enhance and maintain human body parts and organs.

The technology is trying ot evolve and ecompass even more advanced deficiencies such as loss of vision or hearing according to AABME (Alliance of Advanced BioMedical Engineering).
